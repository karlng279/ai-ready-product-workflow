# ST-008: Acceptance Criteria

**Story:** Provider browses repair jobs
**USL Reference:** [ST-008 in usl.md](../usl.md#st-008-provider-browses-repair-jobs)
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-03

---

## UI Elements

- **AC-001:** "Repair Jobs" feed displaying open requests.
- **AC-002:** Filters for: Location, Urgency, Container Type.
- **AC-003:** Job Card showing summary: Location, Damage Type, Time posted.

---

## UI Behavior

- **AC-004:** Clicking a job opens the Job Detail view with full photos.
- **AC-005:** Infinite scroll or pagination for job feed.

---

## Logic

- **AC-006:** Default sorts by distance to provider's registered location (if available) or newest first.
- **AC-007:** Closed/Assigned jobs are hidden from the feed.

---

## Special Notes

- **AC-008:** Provider must be "Verified" to view contact details (hidden in MVP until bid accepted).

---

## Non-Functional Requirements

- **NFR-001:** Feed load time < 1s.
