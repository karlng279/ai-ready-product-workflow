# ST-007: Acceptance Criteria

**Story:** Owner posts maintenance request
**USL Reference:** [ST-007 in usl.md](../usl.md#st-007-owner-posts-maintenance-request)
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-03

---

## UI Elements

- **AC-001:** "Request Repair" page has form fields: Container ID, Location (Port/Depot), Damage Description, Preferred Date.
- **AC-002:** Photo upload section for damage evidence.
- **AC-003:** "Submit Request" button.

---

## UI Behavior

- **AC-004:** Auto-complete/dropdown for Location based on common ports.
- **AC-005:** Submit redirects to "My Maintenance Requests" dashboard.

---

## Logic

- **AC-006:** Damage description minimum 20 characters.
- **AC-007:** At least one photo is required.

---

## Special Notes

- **AC-008:** Geolocation tagging for the request is nice-to-have but optional for MVP.

---

## Non-Functional Requirements

- **NFR-001:** Photo upload reliable on mobile networks (retry mechanism).
